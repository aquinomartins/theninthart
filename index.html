Manifesto

Every artistic language evolves.

For centuries, every artistic language has evolved through new techniques, new theories and new technologies.

Music evolved from notation to harmony.
Architecture evolved from geometry to engineering.
Cinema evolved from photography to digital media.

The Ninth Art is still only beginning.

The Ninth Art is an international initiative dedicated to advancing comics as a complete language for narrative, participation, interaction and human communication.

We believe the future of comics is not only to tell stories — but to design new ways for people to think, participate and imagine together.

Mission

Our Mission

To establish the Ninth Art as a first-class language through research, education, technology and international collaboration.
